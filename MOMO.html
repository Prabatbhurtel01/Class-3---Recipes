MO MO

Description

Nepali momo is a type of dumpling filled with a savory mixture of minced meat and vegetables, wrapped in thin dough and steamed to perfection. Served hot with a spicy dipping sauce, momos are a popular and delicious snack or appetizer enjoyed in Nepali cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Minced chicken or pork
  • Finely chopped onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
