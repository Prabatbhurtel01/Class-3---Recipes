Chicken Pizza

Description

Chicken Pizza: A mouthwatering blend of seasoned grilled chicken, gooey cheese, and tangy tomato sauce atop a freshly made hand-tossed crust. Each bite offers a perfect balance of flavors and textures, making it an irresistible choice for pizza lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

  • Hand-tossed pizza crust
  • Seasoned grilled chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings such as onions, peppers, mushrooms, or barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza crust packaging.
  2. Spread a layer of Domino's tomato sauce evenly over the prepared pizza crust.
  3. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Arrange the seasoned grilled chicken pieces evenly across the cheese.
  5. Add any optional toppings you desire, such as onions, peppers, mushrooms, or a drizzle of barbecue sauce.
  6. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake according to the instructions on the crust packaging, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
  7. Once baked to perfection, remove the pizza from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
Enjoy your homemade Domino's Chicken Pizza!
